About

Rajvi Ranjit Patil Design Technologist · Entrepreneur · Architect

Originally from Sangli, Maharashtra, India, I trained as an architect and began my journey in the real estate sector in Pune with Ranjit Estates & Developers.

Completed my graduate studies in May 2026 in Design Technology at Cornell University. I am based in New York City. My work explores the intersection of design, technology, and systems thinking, with a focus on translating ideas into tangible systems and prototypes.

Driven by the ambition to build and scale ideas, I am particularly interested in creating solutions that can operate across diverse contexts and geographies, addressing real-world challenges through integrated design and technological approaches.

Focus

Inventing and innovating systems that enable sustainable and emerging futures.

Approach

Challenging existing systems through bold interventions that drive meaningful impact for people and planet.
